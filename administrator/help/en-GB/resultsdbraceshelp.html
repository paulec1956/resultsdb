Managing Races: Add, modify and delete

Click on Races in the list on the left hand side of the page:
  • Add a race
  • Delete a race
  • Modify a race
  • Check how the new race looks

Add a race

  1. Click on Races on the left hand side of the screen to list the races
  2. Click on the green New button to open the input screen for a result.
  3. This opens the form to add a race:
  4. Enter the race details:
    5. Field Details
    Description: Enter the name of the race. To make it easier to find when entering results it may be easier to include the year in the name e.g. 2020 Langley 7
    Additional Info: Add more details about the race e.g. - 13.8 miles 1152ft ascent (1198 ran)
    Date: Choose the date of the race using the Date Picker tool
    Runners: Enter the number of runners in the race overall
    Distance (miles): Enter the race distance in miles
    Ascent (ft): Enter the ascent of the race in feet.
    Race Terrain: Enter Road, Fell, Trail or Cross-Country
    Comment: Add any relevant comments

  5. Click Save and Close to save the race.

Delete a Race

  1. In the list of races select the race to be deleted by ticking the box on the left hand side
  2. Click the Trash button

Modify a race

  1. In the races list click the Name of the race.
  2. This will bring the result details form to be changed.
  3. Click Save & Close to save changes or Close to abort the changes.
